CrypTalk is a web-based encrypted messaging platform developed to prioritise privacy amongst its users. The application was concieved on the opinion that many other social media platforms' tendenices to store an abundance of information on their own users and in order to combat this our developments were spearheaded with the idea of keeping our a users personal information private to not only other users but also us, creating a free, completely safe and privatised system built on the core principles of confidentiality.
